The Quran App is designed to operate entirely offline, without any connection to the internet. Internet and Network Access The Quran App does not require or utilize any internet access. It operates locally on your device, ensuring that: No Data is Sent or Received: The app does not communicate with any external servers or services. There is no exchange of information between the app and external networks. No Third-Party Integrations: The app does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, ensuring full privacy for users.

Security

Since the Quran App operates solely offline, it is highly secure from any external data interception or hacking attempts related to internet connections. Your data and usage are entirely contained within your device, providing a secure environment for personal use.
